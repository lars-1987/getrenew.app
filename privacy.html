Renew ("we", "our", "us") is operated by RenewPro, a sole trader registered in Australia. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

We use your information to:

Data location: Our primary database servers are located in Australia. Some third-party services may process data in other regions (US, EU) in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction — Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your data
  • Portability — Receive your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent — Where processing is based on consent
  • Complaint — Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

For Australian Users

You have rights under the Australian Privacy Act 1988. You can contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au if you have concerns.

For EU/UK Users (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union or United Kingdom, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Our lawful bases for processing are:

  • Contract — To provide the App services you requested
  • Legitimate interests — To improve and secure the App
  • Consent — For optional features like marketing emails (if applicable)

For California Users (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act. We do not sell personal information.

Children's Privacy

Renew is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Marketing Communications

We may send you marketing emails about new features, tips, or promotions. You can opt out at any time by:

  • Clicking "unsubscribe" in any marketing email
  • Updating your preferences in the App settings
  • Contacting us directly

We will always send transactional emails (password resets, payment receipts, important service updates) regardless of your marketing preferences.

Changes to This Policy

Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
